Courses We Offer

S-cube university is a renowned institute offering higher education in the fields of science, engineering, pharmacy and business.

Engineering

B. Tech or Bachelor of Technology is one of the prestigious and most popular courses opted by science students. It is a 4-year full- time undergraduate course and is offered in various disciplines such as Computer Science, Civil, Mechanical, Electronics, and Information Technology.

Business

A master of business administration (MBA) is a graduate degree that provides theoretical and practical training for business or investment management. An MBA is designed to help graduates gain a better understanding of general business management functions.

Pharmacy

Our B.Pharm. programme has been structured to match and exceed the requirements of the Pharmacy Council of India. The programme offers students additional courses which give the shape and flavour of engineering as well as fundamental sciences giving an added dimension to the student’s academics.

Our Facilities

S-CUBE's infrastructure is one of the biggest & best in the World. The campus offers all the modern academic and recreational facilities to its students. The campus is replete with state-of-art infrastructure which includes amenities such as a sports complex, labs, gymnasium, etc.

World Class Library

Our college library typically offers a wide range of facilities to support the academic needs of its students. These include a large collection of books and other print materials, as well as access to digital resources such as online journals and databases. In addition, the library offer study spaces, including quiet areas for individual study and group study rooms for collaboration.Our libraries also have computer labs and printing facilities, as well as other services such as reference assistance and research support.

Largest Play Ground

Our college campus have a variety of recreational facilities, including playgrounds, sports fields, and fitness centers. These facilities are typically designed to provide students with opportunities to engage in physical activity, improve their overall health and wellbeing, and build a sense of community on campus.

Tasty and Healthy

Our college campus have a variety of dining facilities that cater to the diverse needs and preferences of the students. These include cafeterias, coffee shops, fast food outlets, and restaurants that offer a wide range of meal options. Our college also offer meal plans that allow students to purchase food on campus using a prepaid account, which can be a convenient and cost-effective way to access dining facilities.
